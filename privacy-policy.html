WidgyCal

Privacy Policy — Last updated June 2026

WidgyCal is a home-screen calendar widget for Android. This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

What data does WidgyCal access?

WidgyCal requests permission to read your device's calendar (READ_CALENDAR). This is used solely to display your calendar events and event indicators inside the widget on your home screen.

Does WidgyCal collect or store personal data?

No. WidgyCal does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All calendar information is read directly from your device and rendered locally in the widget. Nothing leaves your device.

Does WidgyCal share data with third parties?

No. WidgyCal does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or external servers involved.

Does WidgyCal use the internet?

No. WidgyCal does not require or use an internet connection.

What data is stored on your device?

WidgyCal saves a small set of widget preferences (such as your selected theme and highlight color) in local app storage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
